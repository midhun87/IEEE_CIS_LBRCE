Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) Student Chapter. Our mission is to advance the understanding and application of computational intelligence for the benefit of humanity.

We host a variety of events, including workshops, seminars, and hackathons, to foster a community of learning and innovation. Whether you're a student, a researcher, or a professional, we invite you to explore our work and join our community.

About Computational Intelligence

Computational intelligence (CI) is a set of nature-inspired computational methodologies and approaches to problem solving. The term refers to the ability of a computer to learn from data and apply that learning to new problems. Key areas of CI include:

  • Neural Networks
  • Fuzzy Systems
  • Evolutionary Computation
  • Swarm Intelligence
